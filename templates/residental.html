IBB plan and build your house from A to Z

Rely on the professionalism of IBB Baugesellschaft for the design, planning and construction of your house. Our technicians and the entire IBB team are at your side with all their experience. Whether in the field of consulting, technical construction planning, structural analysis and construction supervision, our employees implement the highest quality standards and accompany you until completion and handover of keys.

We build your living ideas:

  • single-family houses, two-family houses, multi-family houses
  • semi-detached houses and terraced houses
  • two-story or multi-story houses
  • flat roof houses
  • houses with a pitched roof or with a gable roof

IBB act as general contractor and provide turnkey constructions, i.e. all services from one single source:

  • completed structural shell with roof
  • interior woodwork
  • masonry
  • tiling
  • interior equipment (electrical installation, sanitation)
  • facade work and plastering

We put together the right team for all stages of your residential construction and pay attention to a transparent price-performance ratio and the on-time performance of the work.

Architecture & building style - standard is a taboo

We build your house and fulfill individual living dreams - from the classic single-family home to the complex appartment building, from the innovative low-energy house to the chic multi-family house. In doing so, we remain true to our values: responsibility, quality, innovation and an open and transparent approach to our clients.

In order to meet the requirements of our clients, we take over all services. Our client has only one contact person for the entire duration of the project and the subsequent warranty phase. IBB Baugesellschaft is responsible for all trades. The customer can rely on qualified advice in all project phases. Change requests during the ongoing construction are also possible.
